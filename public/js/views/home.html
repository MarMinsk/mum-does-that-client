About Us

Being a mother is one of the most fulfilling and rewarding jobs you can do. It’s also one of the most challenging, for all sorts of reasons. Making the transition from the demands of a professional career to those of being a full-time mum can sometimes leave you feeling like your missing out on making full use of the skills you’ve spent so many years developing.

Mums Does That aims to fill a gap that allows highly skilled professional women who are on career breaks to offer their skills to individuals and organisations that are looking for help on specific tasks in a flexible way. Users can search for and contact other users who have the skills they need or post details of tasks they need completed. The aim is to harness the vast amount of talent that’s available from professional women on career breaks to create a vibrant and diverse community of shared skills and experience.
